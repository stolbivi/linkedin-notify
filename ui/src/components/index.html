Integrated Google Maps

Our Chrome extension seamlessly integrates location information directly into the candidate's profile, allowing you to easily view their location and navigate Google Maps directly from within LinkedIn.

Find Local Time

Last Viewed

Eliminate the need to repeatedly review users you've already analyzed. Our Chrome extension remembers the last time you visited a profile, so you can easily pick up where you left off and avoid unnecessary duplication of effort.

Auto Services

Do you find yourself spending hours manually liking and reposting posts from colleagues to increase their visibility and expand your network? Our Chrome extension can help. Simply enable the Auto Like or Auto Repost buttons for a user, and all their future posts will be automatically liked and reposted, saving you valuable time and effort.
