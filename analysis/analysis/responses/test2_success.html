        Glassdoor Economist Pay Overview

        What is the pay outlook for Research & Science?

        Research and science jobs offer pay well above the national average. Increased demand for STEM workers is increasing salaries in research and science. Jobs in these fields, however, often require higher education at the graduate level and/or specialized training. While domain-specific expertise gives workers in these fields access to very specialized jobs, there are also many employers who hire STEM workers for their general data and technology skills.

        How does the Security researcher salary in San Francisco, CA compare to the national average salary in US for the same job?

        The average salary for a Security researcher in US is $124,072 per year based on 147 salary contributions, which is 7.19% less than the salary for a Security researcher in San Francisco, CA. The cost of living and the local demand for the work are among the factors affecting wages. The top 3 companies in US where a Security researcher gets the highest compensation are BlackBerry, Novetta and CrowdStrike. Bigger companies typically pay 17.48% less than smaller companies for a Security researcher.
